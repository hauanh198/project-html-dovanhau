How it Works

Safe-X Token

$SAFEX is the native token which interest rebase rewards are paid. Every token holder automatically receives 0.023948% interest every 15 minutes just for holding $SAFEX tokens in their own wallet!

Auto-Compounding

Crypto’s Highest Paying Auto-Staking and Auto-Compounding Protocol with the greatest fixed APY in the industry of 440,346.71% Interest rewards are compounded every 15 minutes for every BSC wallet holding any $SAFEX tokens.

Safe-X Insurance Fund(SXIF)

The SXIF serves as an insurance fund to achieve price stability and longterm sustainability of the Safe-X Protocol by maintaining a consistent 0.023948% rebase rate paid to all $SAFEX token holders.

Safe-X Treasury

The Treasury provides support to the SXIF in the event of an extreme price drop in the $SAFEX token. The Treasury also funds investments, new Safe-X projects and marketing for $SAFEX.

The Burn Pit

2.5% of all $SAFEX traded are burnt in the Burn Pit. The more that is traded, the more get put into the fire causing the fire pit to grow in size reducing the circulating supply and keeping the Safe-X protocol stable.
